Who I am

  • Who you are?

    My name is Claudia Mastromauro, I’m an italian account executive of 27 years old and I moved here in London from Italy with the aim to live an international professional (and personal) experience.

  • How you can describe yourself?

    Quick to absorb and analyse information, imaginative, enthusiastic, energetic and outgoing, approachable, diplomatic and flexible, a clear communicator, in person and writing, interested in people and what motivates their choices.

  • How would you outline your professional profile?

    Basically, my role is to be the client representative within the advertising/web agency, and the agency representative to the client. I meet with clients and talk through what they want from their campaign (on line, above the line or below: any kind of communication/advertising project it’s in their minds), who they want to target, how much they have available to invest, and their objectives, such as raising brand awareness or increasing sales. I then do any necessary background research and produce a brief to present to the creative and technical teams. Then, with the creative department, develop and implement the campaign.

  • What skills do you have?

    Good communication skills are essential as taking in and passing on information is a key function of my job. Because it’s such a fast-paced environment, it’s also important I’m able to manage your time effectively and prioritise tasks. There is an element of creativity in the role; certainly being able to recognise good creative work is important, and it also helps to really enjoy the work and be able to have fun with it.

  • Do you use any special tools or equipment?

    My computer and my mobile phone are my main tools. I use my computer to prepare presentations and produce spreadsheets and other documents. It also has a finance system, as one of my responsibilities as account handler is to set and organise the budgets, raising purchase orders and invoicing the client. Also I’m always available to speak with my clients by phone and to check my e-mail account (almost 7/24).

  • What do you like about your job?

    It’s extremely varied, both in the tasks I have to complete and the clients that I deal with. For me every new project is a new challange and the key of access to a new world.
